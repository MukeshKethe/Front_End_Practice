Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, often referred to as CR7, is a Portuguese professional footballer born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Standing 6 feet 2 inches tall, Ronaldo plays as a forward and is known for his incredible speed, skill, and goal-scoring ability. He began his career with Sporting CP before moving to top clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and currently Al-Nassr. He is a five-time Ballon d'Or winner and holds the record as the all-time leading scorer in the UEFA Champions League. On the international stage, Ronaldo has represented Portugal since 2003, leading them to victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship and the 2019 UEFA Nations League. Off the pitch, he is a successful entrepreneur with his CR7 brand and is renowned for his philanthropic efforts.
